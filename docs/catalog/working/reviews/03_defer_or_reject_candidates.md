# Advice Contamination Review

Identify catalog content that stops being a mirror.

Reject when:
- `next_action`, `recommended_decision`, `decision_options`, `validation_plan`, `rollback_condition`, `failure_conditions`, `allowed_files`, or `max_files_changed` appears.
- meaning tells the reader what to do instead of describing the current implication.
- fact includes inference words such as likely, possible, risk, should, or ought.
- the draft asks the reviewer to accept a plan instead of checking fact and meaning quality.
